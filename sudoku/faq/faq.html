Frequently Asked Questions

Sudoku365 Classic FAQ

Quick answers for common gameplay questions, support issues, and troubleshooting steps.

Question 01

How do I reset a puzzle?

To reset a puzzle, you have to start a new sudoku.

Question 02

My puzzle is not registering as completed

There will be feedback when all numbers of the sudoku are filled in, either a win screen or an incomplete modal will appear.

Question 03

Can I track my completed puzzles?

Right now there is no way to keep track of the number of completed sudokus other than completed sudokus in a streak, but it might come in a future update.

Question 04

How do I report a bug?

Please use our GitHub Issues page to report bugs: Report a Bug or email me at devdellsstudio@gmail.com.

Question 05

Why do some puzzles feel harder than others in the same difficulty?

Each sudoku is randomly selected from a large set of puzzles. While they are grouped by difficulty, there can still be natural variation in challenge.

Question 06

Does Sudoku365 Classic work offline?

Yes, once installed, you can play all puzzles offline without needing an internet connection.

Question 07

Can I turn off the sound effects?

Yes, you can disable sound effects in the settings screen.

Question 08

How do I change the theme?

You can change the theme in the settings screen. Choose from light and dark theme.

Still need help?

If your issue is not covered here, include the app version, device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
