Frequently Asked Questions

Calculator FAQ

Quick answers for common calculator questions, support issues, and troubleshooting.

Question 01

How do I clear the current calculation?

Use the clear button in the calculator interface to reset the current input.

Question 02

Why is the result different from what I expected?

Check the order of operations and the numbers you entered. If the issue persists, report it with the exact steps and values used.

Question 03

Does the calculator work offline?

Yes, the app is designed to work without an internet connection once it is installed.

Question 04

How do I report a bug?

Please use the GitHub Issues page to report bugs: Report a Bug or email me at devdellsstudio@gmail.com.

Question 05

Does the app store any personal information?

No personal account system is required. See the privacy page for the full policy.

Still need help?

If your issue is not covered here, include the app version, device model, iOS version, and the exact numbers or operation you entered.
