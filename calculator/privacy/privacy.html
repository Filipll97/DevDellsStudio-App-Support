SimplyACalculator Privacy Policy

A clear overview of what data the SimplyACalculator stores locally and how support contact is handled.

Last updated: 2026-04-10

Data

Data Collection

SimplyACalculator does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Calculations and preferences stay on your device unless a future app feature says otherwise.

Contact

Direct Contact

If you contact me directly by email, I may receive your email address and any information you include in your message. This is only used to respond to your inquiry.

Public Reports

GitHub Issues

For bug reports and feature requests, users may choose to open an issue on the public GitHub repository. Please note that information you provide in a GitHub issue, including your username and submitted content, is public and visible to others. Review GitHub's Privacy Policy for platform details.
